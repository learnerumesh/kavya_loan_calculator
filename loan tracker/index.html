Think it through!

Family is more invested in your well-being, making them a reliable choice for emergency funds. Borrowing from friends may strain relationships, especially with the expectation of quick repayment.

Think before asking!

Pause and Ponder

Selling antique furniture and heirlooms during an emergency often leads to significant financial loss, as buyers in such situations take advantage of your urgency to offer below-market prices. Additionally, these items hold sentimental and long-term value, which may far outweigh the immediate financial benefit of selling them.

Think before selling!

Decision Checkpoint!

Before choosing, analyze the total cost of the loan over its entire duration, including interest, processing fees, and other charges. A slightly higher interest rate might be more cost-effective if the upfront fees are significantly lower.

Think before choosing!
